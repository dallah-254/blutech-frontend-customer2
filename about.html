Who We Are

Blue Technology is Nairobi's premier destination for all your technology needs. Since 2010, we've been providing exceptional repair services, quality products, and expert advice to thousands of satisfied customers.

Our team of certified technicians specializes in repairing smartphones, laptops, tablets, and other electronic devices. We use only genuine parts and offer competitive pricing with a warranty on all our services.

Beyond repairs, we offer a wide selection of new and refurbished devices, accessories, and tech solutions tailored to meet your needs and budget.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality technology repair and sales services that exceed customer expectations while building lasting relationships based on trust and excellence.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted and innovative technology service provider, known for exceptional customer service, technical expertise, and commitment to sustainability.
